1) INFORMATIONS REGARDING THE SURVEY OF PERSONALIZED DATA AND CONTACT DATA OF THE PERSON RESPONSIBLE

1.1 We are honored that you want to use our application. In this document we will inform you about the usage of your personalized data while using our application. Personalized data is all data that you can be personally identified with.

1.2 The E-Mail address of the person responsible for the data processing of this application in the context of the General Data Protection Regulation GDPR is johannes.weih@web.de.

2) AQUSITION OF DATA DURING APPLICATION USAGE

All data is exclusively stored locally on your device and is never shared with us or anyone else.

2.1 Creating/modifying a document

When you create or modify a document, the name is stored locally on your device.

2.2 Adding a page to a document

When you scan a page with the camera and add it to a document, the image data is stored locally on your device. The permission to access your devices camera is required for this purpose.

3) STORE DURATION OF PERSONALIZED DATA

The data is stored on your device, until you uninstall the application or manually delete the data.
